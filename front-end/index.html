About us

UniConnect is a student hub created by students, for students. We built this platform to solve the everyday challenges of campus life - from finding study groups to recovering lost items. Our mission is to create a more connected campus community where students can easily share resources, support each other, and make university life more organized and collaborative. We believe in making student life simpler, together.
