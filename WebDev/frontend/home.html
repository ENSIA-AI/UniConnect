What is UniConnect?

UniConnect is a comprehensive, centralized platform designed specifically for students to streamline their academic and campus life. We understand the challenges students face in staying organized, finding study partners, managing resources, and connecting with their campus community. That's why we've created a single, unified hub where you can access everything you need to succeed.

Our platform bridges the gap between students, making it easier than ever to collaborate, share knowledge, and support one another throughout your academic journey. Whether you're looking for lost items, selling textbooks, finding study groups, or organizing your tasks, UniConnect has you covered.

Our Features & Services

Lost & Found Board

Never lose hope of finding your missing items again! Our Lost & Found Board is a dedicated space where students can report items they've lost or found on campus. Each post includes a detailed description, category classification (such as Books, Electronics, Clothing, or Personal Items), and optional images to help identify the item. The platform makes it easy to search through listings and reunite students with their belongings quickly and efficiently.

Quick Search Categories Image Upload

Study Group Finder

Studying alone can be challenging, but finding the right study partners shouldn't be! Our Study Group Finder connects students based on courses, modules, and study goals. You can create or join study groups, specify your preferred meeting times, share your study focus areas, and coordinate with classmates. Whether you're preparing for exams, working on projects, or just need help understanding complex topics, this feature helps you find motivated peers to study with.

Course Matching Time Coordination Goal Setting

Student Marketplace

Save money and reduce waste with our student-to-student marketplace! Buy, sell, or trade textbooks, electronics, dorm essentials, furniture, and more. Each listing includes detailed descriptions, pricing information, condition ratings, and images. Whether you're looking to sell last semester's books, find affordable study materials, or upgrade your dorm room on a budget, our marketplace makes peer-to-peer transactions safe, simple, and convenient.

Buy & Sell Trade Items Student Prices

Resource Sharing Hub

Access a vast library of student-contributed academic resources! Upload and download study materials including lecture notes, past exam papers, assignment solutions, code repositories, research papers, and study guides. All resources are organized by subject and course, making it easy to find exactly what you need. Contribute your own materials to help fellow students while building a collaborative learning community.

Notes Past Exams Study Guides

Smart To-Do List

Stay organized and never miss a deadline with our intelligent task management system! Create, prioritize, and track your assignments, projects, exams, and personal goals all in one place. Set reminders, organize tasks by course or category, mark priorities, and check off completed items. The visual progress tracking helps you stay motivated and ensures you're always on top of your academic responsibilities.

Task Priority Reminders Progress Tracking
